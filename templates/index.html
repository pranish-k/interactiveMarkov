Interactive Markov Market Analysis

Exploring the Markov Property in S&P 500 Market Behavior (2015-2024)

This interactive tool demonstrates empirical findings from comprehensive Markov chain analysis of U.S. stock market behavior. Discover why markets exhibit the Markov property (one-week memory) but fail time homogeneity, making them unpredictable despite being memoryless.

Key Research Finding

Markets pass the Markov property test (χ² p-value > 0.05) indicating one-week memory, but fail time homogeneity as transition probabilities change dramatically across periods, resulting in poor prediction accuracy (~35%) despite statistically valid models.
